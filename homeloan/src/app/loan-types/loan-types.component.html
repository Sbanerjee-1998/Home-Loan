TYPES OF HOME LOANS GRANTED

A housing loan is a specified sum of money borrowed from banks and Housing Finance Companies (HFCs) to purchase a house. Important factors to be considered while seeking a house loan are interest rate, loan amount, loan tenure, monthly EMIs on house loan and credit score.

Home Purchase Loans

This type of loan is taken to buy a residential property, including flats, row houses and bungalows. The maximum loan amount offered is 90% of the present market value of the property.

Eligibility Criteria

Particulars Salaried Professionals Self Employed Indiviuals
Age 18 – 65 years 18 – 65 years
Employment Min. 2 years of experience Min. 5 years of experience
Credit Score 750 or above 750 or above

Documents Required

  • Age Proof(Birth certificate, Class 10 marks sheet)
  • Address Proof (Copy of passport, Voter ID)
  • ncome Proof (Salary slips and ITR details)
  • Identification Proof (PAN card, Passport, Voter ID, etc.)
  • Documents of Property to be Purchased

Home Improvement Loans

This type of home loan can be availed to renovate, repair or refurnish your home. Home improvement loans are very popular due to low interest rate.

Eligibility Criteria

Age Employment
18 – 65 years
  • Salaried Individuals
  • Self-Employed: Available for both Professionals and Non-Professionals

Documents Required

  • Age Proof(Birth certificate, Class 10 marks sheet)
  • Address Proof (Copy of passport, Voter ID)
  • Income Proof (Salary slips and ITR details)
  • Estimated Renovation Quotation
  • Property Title Deeds (Original)
